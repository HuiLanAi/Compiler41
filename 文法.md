LOGIC -> SYNX COMPARE SYNX | SYNX COMPARE NUMBER | SYNX COMPARE ARITH | SYNX | NUMBER | NOT SYNX | NUMBER COMPARE SYNX |           NUMBER COMPARE NUMBER 
         a < b             | a == 2.3             | a == b * 1.2   | if(a)     | if(1)  | if(!a)   | if(2 == a)          | if(2 < 3)
